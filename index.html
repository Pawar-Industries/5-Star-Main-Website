About Us

Our Purpose

5 Star Business Association was founded for the purpose of bringing suppliers (“partners”) and member businesses ("members") together for mutual gain. We negotiate special pricing with our partners for products our members frequently buy. We make those savings available to our members for a small monthly membership fee. This in turn helps our partners widen their customer base and increase sales.


Background

Our parent company, 5 Star Business Solutions, has been serving the restaurant and retail industry for years with point-of-sale and credit card processing solutions. It seemed a natural extension of the business to offer additional value to our customers in the form of negotiated discounts. Hence 5 Star Business Association was born.

If you’d like to learn more about becoming a member, or increasing your customer base and revenue by becoming a partner, please contact us today.
